Taylor Ward

www.taylorcward.com

Taylor is a curious experience designer who is passionate about film, visual design and brand strategy. Behind the scenes, he was a context cinematopher. Taylor would move through the design studios seeking a series of different angles and perspectives in order to achieve a visual narrative for the interviews. He, alongside Roxanne Henschke, conducted interviews for Maria Sebregondi of Moleskine, Giulio Iacchetti, and Andrea Ponsi. Post-field, he spent most of his time editing, subtitling and eating tacos.
