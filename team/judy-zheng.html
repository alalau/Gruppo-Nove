Judy Zheng

www.judyzheng.ca

Judy is a visual designer and has a love for all things that capture the eye. She especially enjoys discovering mundane surroundings and conceptualizing them into graphical experiences. In-field, Judy acted as the cinematographer and was responsible for directing and framing shots. Alongside Juan, she led interviews with typographer and graphic designer, Marta Bernstein and architect, designer Fabio Novembre. She also created the brand and identity for this year's field school and assisted in the art direction of the website. She is also super proud of herself for making it through the trip despite being hypersensitive to dairy (Italians do love their cheese).
