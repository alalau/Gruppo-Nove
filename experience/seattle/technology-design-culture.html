Seattle Featurette

Featuring: Jay Deguchi of the architectural firm Suyama Peterson Deguchi, Heather Pleschett of Knoll Inc. David Butler of Le Caviste, and Josh Hayword of Digital Kitchen.

We asked our interviewees to speculate on the growing technology scene in Seattle from their respective industries. This film is a collection of answers from an architect, a sales manager for a workspace furniture company, a wine bar owner and a creative director of a digital design agency in response to the question: What is Seattle?
