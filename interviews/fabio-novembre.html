  • Fabio Novembre

    Fabio Novembre is an architect and a designer. Born in Lecce, Fabio moved to Milan in 1984 to study architecture at the Politecnico di Milano. He carved out his own path in design, guided by instinct, and created a name for himself through his stimulating and culturally sensitive commercial works. His signature Italian flair has brought him international recognition and he has since worked with Italian design giants Cappellini, Flaminia and Casamania, as well as fashion brands Stuart Weitzman and Tardini. Currently he is working on the interior design and architecture of Casa Milan, the headquarters of the AC Milan soccer team.
