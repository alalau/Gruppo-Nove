Francesca Lanzavecchia

Francesca Lanzavecchia is one half of Lanzavecchia+Wai, a studio based in Italy and Singapore. She was trained as an industrial designer in polytechnico di milano. She then earned her masters degree in design academy in Eindhoven, which was where she met Hunn Wai, her partner in the studio. The studio’s projects reflect a need to speak about cultural problems. Their process is deeply oriented in empathy, human context and cultural research, occaisonally collaborating with traditional craftsmen. Recently, the studio was awarded the title “Best Young Talent” of 2014 by Elle Decor.

“You need to know culture to make something that is relevant. You want to make something that is relevant for the life of today and not just something for the tourists of yesterday.”

"Design tells a story that is not the truth. Design tells a story of bodies that are average bodies. Design tells a story of perfection. We are in love with imperfection. We are in love with the fragility of human beings"

This interview was conducted by Liam Leyland and Sonya Spowage
