  • Marco Zanuso Jr.

    Marco Zanuso Jr. is an architect and a designer. He grew up around design since his uncle Marco Zanuso, an architect and designer, was regarded as a leading figure in modern post-war italian design. In the past, he’s worked with some of Italy’s greatest designers Enzo Mari and Achille Castiglioni at the italian design university Polytechnico di Milano. His own designs are heavily influenced by radicalism and the memphis movement. Recently, he has collaborated with small producers to create projects that experiment with traditional italian craftsmanship.

  • "There in an influence of Memphis in everything."

  • “This process of change, modification, getting better or different can be with no end.”

This interview was conducted by Scott Horsfall and Terrence Ma
