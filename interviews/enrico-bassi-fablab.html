  • Enrico Bassi

    Enrico Bassi is the facilitator at the Fablab in Turin. The Fablab was set up by arduino to allow all makers a place to bring their ideas to life.

  • “In design making mistakes is essential because you can’t be sure about something until you tried it and failed.”

  • "The power of sharing is that the idea is just the beginning. The point is to bring as many ideas as possible to reality and you usually you can’t do it by yourself”
