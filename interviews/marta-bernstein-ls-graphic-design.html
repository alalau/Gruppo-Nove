Marta Bernstein

Marta Bernstein is a graphic and type designer. She co-founded the Milan-based studio "LS Graphic Design” in 2006, while receiving her masters of design at the Royal Academy of Arts in the Netherlands. During her masters, she developed an editorial type family called “Alice” -- a typeface which receives it’s personality from it's broad, eye-friendly proportions without being overwhelming on the page. Her studio has done work for a wide variety of clients from NGO’s to public institutions to international companies.

"To me choosing a typeface and choosing how to use it is somehow shaping the identity of the project.”
