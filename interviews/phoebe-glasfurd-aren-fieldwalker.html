  • Phoebe Glasfurd &

    Phoebe Glasfurd and Aren Fieldwalker are the two founders of Glasfurd & Walker, an award-winning design studio that specializes in brand creation, graphic design, and art direction. Her, the Creative Director. Him, the Managing Director. They're best known for designing brand experiences for some of Vancouver's most beloved (and most delicious) eateries, such as Meat & Bread, The Juice Truck, and Bao Bei Brasserie to name a few.
